Overview

AIdentify is our data science project, built in less than 2 weeks, which, given a live video input, identifies faces and classifies the emotions being expressed . The project intends to demonstrate numerous facets of data science, from data preparation to model selection and training. There are numerous potential applications of our project, including measuring reactions to digital advertisements, improving eLearning, and getting feedback on digital entertainment experiences.

This website was built as a way to showcase our entire process and thereby hopefully also teach you about certain aspects of data science and machine learning (if you didnâ€™t know about them already)!

In the next few pages we will explain our workflow: from data retrieval to data preparation to modeling, training, and model evaluation. At the very end you will be able to try for yourself the magic of machine learning by testing our final product!
